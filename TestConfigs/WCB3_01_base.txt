*** Identity
***   ?HW,23          Hardware version 2.3
***   ?WCB,3          This is WCB3
***   ?WCBQ,3         3 boards in system
***   ?MAC,2,D3       2nd MAC group octet
***   ?MAC,3,3B       3rd MAC group octet
***   ?EPASS,Jq6SECq8dsNRmq   ESP-NOW password
***
*** ETM
***   ?ETM,ON             Enable ETM
***   ?ETM,HB,10          Heartbeat every 10 seconds
***   ?ETM,MISS,3         3 missed = board offline
***   ?ETM,TIMEOUT,500    500ms ACK wait before retry
***   ?ETM,CHKSM,ON       Checksum enabled
***
*** Serial baud rates (hardware-appropriate even without device config)
***   ?BAUD,S1,57600      S1 Maestro ID3 (Maestro requires 57600)
***   ?BAUD,S2,9600       S2 Marcduino Clone (MP3 Trigger source)
***   ?BAUD,S3,9600       S3 Marcduino Clone Serial Interface

***   ?BAUD,S5,9600       S5 Servo 4 PWM Output

*** Labels

***   ?LABEL,S3,Marcduino Serial
***   ?LABEL,CLEAR,S4
***   ?LABEL,S5,Servo 4 PWM Out

*** Reset broadcast to all-on defaults
***   ?BCAST,RESET

*** Clear all device configs and mappings
